# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis (Tableau)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior using a telecom dataset. The objective is to identify why customers leave a service and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue in subscription-based businesses.
This analysis helps answer:

* Why are customers leaving?
* Which customer segments are most at risk?
* How can retention be improved?

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Tableau (for dashboard & visualization)
* Excel (for basic data handling)

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle)
* Contains customer demographics, subscription details, and churn status

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

* Churn rate analysis
* Customer segmentation (Contract, Tenure, Charges)
* Tenure-based churn trends
* Pricing impact on churn

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📌 Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show the highest churn
* 📌 **New customers (0–12 months)** are more likely to leave
* 📌 Customers with **higher monthly charges** tend to churn more
* 📌 Long-term contracts significantly improve retention

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* ✅ Encourage long-term subscriptions with discounts
* ✅ Improve onboarding experience for new users
* ✅ Provide personalized pricing plans
* ✅ Target high-risk customers with retention strategies
